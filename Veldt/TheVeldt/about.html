The Veldt (1950)

Published in September of 1950, under the name "The World That The Children Made" for The Saturday Evening Post. The story renamed as The Veldt, is about a family that lives in a house with advanced technology. This house feeds, bathes them, cleans itself, and does anything that its inhabitants wants of it. There is also the nursery, which takes the form of any place that whoever is in it think of. The family deals with the problems of technology and feel that it is taking over their lives and that they are loosing their purpose.

Ray Bradbury (1920-2012)
